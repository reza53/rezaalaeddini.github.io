Forensic DNA analysis entails analyzing DNA samples from crime scenes and suspects to generate distinctive genetic profiles. These profiles are used for comparison to help identify perpetrators, victims, or biological relationships, making DNA analysis a crucial tool in criminal investigations and legal cases.
